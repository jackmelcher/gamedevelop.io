About

GameDevelop.io's mission is to provide Game Developers of any skill level with the resources and knowledgenecessary to develop Video Games.

The Resource Database is designed to be a complete and up to date collection of tools, assets, communities, and services commonly used by game developers.

The Guides are designed to contain critical information for tackling both the production and business sides of game development.

History

GameDevelop.io was originally conceived as a job aggregator for the Video Game Industry. After discovering several well made aggregators, it became apparent that there wasn't any well maintained databases for game development resoruces. So, GameDevelop.io pivoted to be a Resource Database for game development.

To compliment the resource database, Guides were written to help indie developers learn more about other aspects of game development, such as production, business, and marketing.

GameDevelop.io plans to continue expanding the resource database, and to provide insightful and helpful guides.

In the future, GameDevelop.io will provide its own set of tools to help developers come together and collaborate remotely.
